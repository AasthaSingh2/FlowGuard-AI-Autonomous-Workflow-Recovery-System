# FlowGuard AI Impact Model

## Overview

This document outlines the operating impact model for FlowGuard AI - Autonomous Workflow Recovery System. The goal of the model is to show how autonomous workflow verification and recovery can reduce onboarding turnaround time, lower HR effort, and create measurable business value at scale.

The calculations below are based on the current employee onboarding demo and are intended for hackathon evaluation, stakeholder discussion, and business case framing.

## Assumptions

The following assumptions are used for the current impact model:

- Manual onboarding turnaround time: `3 days`
- AI-assisted onboarding turnaround time: `4 hours`
- Manual HR effort per hire: `5 hours`
- AI-assisted HR effort per hire: `1 hour`
- Hiring volume: `100 hires per month`
- Estimated monthly savings: `Rs.2,00,000+`

These assumptions represent a document-driven onboarding workflow where delays are commonly caused by missing data, manual verification, and repeated follow-ups.

## Calculation Logic

The impact model is based on two core value drivers:

- reduction in workflow turnaround time
- reduction in manual HR effort per hire

### Turnaround Time Comparison

- Manual process: `3 days = 72 hours`
- FlowGuard AI process: `4 hours`
- Time saved per onboarding case: `68 hours`

### HR Effort Comparison

- Manual HR effort per hire: `5 hours`
- AI-assisted HR effort per hire: `1 hour`
- Labor saved per hire: `4 hours`

### Monthly Volume Logic

For `100 hires per month`:

- Total HR hours in manual model: `100 x 5 = 500 hours`
- Total HR hours in AI-assisted model: `100 x 1 = 100 hours`
- Total HR hours saved per month: `400 hours`

This means the system can remove a significant amount of repetitive operational effort while also accelerating workflow completion.

## Time Savings

FlowGuard AI reduces onboarding turnaround time from `3 days` to `4 hours`.

### Per Hire

- Manual turnaround time: `72 hours`
- AI-assisted turnaround time: `4 hours`
- Time saved per hire: `68 hours`

### For 100 Hires Per Month

- Aggregate turnaround reduction potential: `6,800 hours` of cycle-time improvement across workflows

This does not mean all 6,800 hours become labor savings. Instead, it reflects faster process completion, lower waiting time, fewer bottlenecks, and quicker readiness for downstream operations such as IT provisioning, payroll setup, and compliance approvals.

## Labor Savings

The strongest direct operating benefit comes from reduced manual HR effort.

### Per Hire

- Manual HR work: `5 hours`
- FlowGuard AI-assisted HR work: `1 hour`
- Labor saved: `4 hours per hire`

### For 100 Hires Per Month

- Manual HR workload: `500 hours`
- AI-assisted HR workload: `100 hours`
- HR effort saved: `400 hours per month`

This saved time can be redirected toward:

- candidate engagement
- exception handling
- policy review
- employee experience improvement
- higher-value strategic HR work

## Business Value

Based on the above assumptions, the estimated business value is:

- Estimated monthly savings: `Rs.2,00,000+` for `100 hires`

This value can be justified through a combination of:

- lower manual processing cost
- reduced back-and-forth on incomplete data
- faster workflow completion
- fewer delays in downstream onboarding tasks
- better utilization of HR operations bandwidth

Even with conservative assumptions, the model demonstrates that workflow recovery automation can create meaningful returns in high-volume onboarding environments.

## Scalability Potential

The value of FlowGuard AI grows with workflow volume. As hiring or transaction count increases, the benefits scale in a largely linear way for repetitive process steps.

### Why It Scales Well

- verification logic can be reused across large numbers of workflows
- recovery actions can be standardized for common failure types
- audit logging remains structured and traceable across volume
- human intervention is reserved for true exceptions

### Expansion Potential

The same model can be applied beyond employee onboarding to:

- vendor onboarding
- procurement workflows
- KYC and customer verification
- claims and document processing
- compliance-driven approval flows

As workflow count increases, the impact compounds through:

- faster cycle times
- reduced operational overhead
- improved throughput
- stronger process consistency

## Summary

FlowGuard AI demonstrates a strong operational case for autonomous workflow recovery:

- turnaround time reduced from `3 days` to `4 hours`
- HR manual effort reduced from `5 hours` to `1 hour`
- approximately `400 HR hours` saved per month for `100 hires`
- estimated monthly savings of `Rs.2,00,000+`

This makes FlowGuard AI not only a compelling hackathon prototype, but also a credible foundation for enterprise workflow automation at scale.
